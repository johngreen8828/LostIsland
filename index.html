Lost Island Hunt Club and Lodge are located on the bank of the Platte River near Linwood Ne., about an hour drive from Omaha or Lincoln, and a short flight upstream from one of the largest waterfowl refuges in the central flyway. With the beautiful Platte River, our enhanced wetlands, and secluded timber at our doorstep, your duck blind or deer stand is just a few minutes from our comfortable lodge.
