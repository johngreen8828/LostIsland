Our waterfowl hunting takes place from heated custom built blinds and pits designed to give you the perfect vantage point to view, harvest, and enjoy decoying ducks and geese, while concealing you safely and comfortably. The pit and permanent blinds on our wetlands are equipped with heat, hot plates for cooking, electric outlets, and comfortable office style chairs. Ice-out systems in our wetlands assure you will have open water even in the harshest conditions. Hunting the sandbars and islands in the Platte River requires mobility, therefore our floating and portable blinds are a bit more spartan, yet still heated and comfortable. Our professional guides, have many years of experience, are expert callers, and will take great care to provide the best and safest possible experience for you and your guests.
